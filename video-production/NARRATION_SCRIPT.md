## VOICEOVER RECORDING DIRECTIONS

### Pre-Recording Setup

**Microphone:**
- Condenser mic (Rode NT1, Audio-Technica AT2020, or better)
- Pop filter required
- Distance: 6-8 inches from mouth
- Angle: Slightly off-axis to reduce plosives

**Recording Environment:**
- Quiet room (bedroom, closet, or studio)
- Minimal echo (use blankets/foam if needed)
- No background noise (AC off, door closed)

**Recording Settings:**
- Sample Rate: 48kHz
- Bit Depth: 24-bit
- Format: WAV (uncompressed)
- Mono channel

**Pre-Recording Checklist:**
- [ ] Hydrate (room temperature water, no ice)
- [ ] Warm up voice (5 min vocal exercises)
- [ ] Read script aloud 2-3 times (familiarize)
- [ ] Clear throat, relax jaw
- [ ] Test record 10 seconds (check levels)

---

### Recording Process

**Levels:**
- Peak: -12dB to -6dB (leave headroom)
- Never clip/distort (red peaks)
- Consistent volume throughout

**Takes:**
- Record entire script 3 times (full takes)
- Record difficult sections separately (pickups)
- Label takes: "AE_VO_Take1.wav", "Take2", "Take3"

**Between Takes:**
- 2-3 minute break
- Hydrate
- Re-read script
- Adjust delivery based on previous take

**Performance Tips:**
- Stand while recording (better breath control)
- Smile on warm sections (hear it in voice)
- Use hand gestures (adds natural energy)
- Visualize the visuals (sync emotion to content)
- Vary tone between sections (don't be monotone)

### Post-Recording Editing

**Cleanup:**
- Remove mouth clicks (RX or iZotope)
- Remove breaths (keep natural ones)
- Cut silence before/after each take
- Normalize to -6dB peak

**Timing Adjustment:**
- Cut pauses to match timing marks
- Use time-stretch for minor adjustments (±5%)
- Ensure sync with visual cues

**Processing Chain:**
1. **EQ (Equalization):**
   - High-pass filter: 80Hz (remove rumble)
   - Boost: 3-5kHz (+2dB for clarity)
   - De-ess: 6-8kHz (reduce sibilance)

2. **Compression:**
   - Ratio: 3:1
   - Threshold: -18dB
   - Attack: 10ms
   - Release: 100ms
   - Gain: +3dB

3. **De-esser:**
   - Target: 6-9kHz
   - Reduction: 4-6dB

4. **Limiter:**
   - Ceiling: -1dB
   - Prevent clipping

**Export Settings:**
- Format: WAV or AIFF
- Sample Rate: 48kHz
- Bit Depth: 24-bit
- Mono
- Filename: "AE_Voiceover_Final_48k_24bit.wav"
